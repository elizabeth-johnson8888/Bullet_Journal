What is bullet journaling?

Bullet Journaling is a type of journaling where you can keep track of events, notes, and other things in an organized, and pretty way. Most people use bright colors and small pictures to decorate the pages of the journal.


Benefits of Journaling

Keeping track of all your things in one place will allow you to gain time management skills and organizational skills. It allows for a safe space to focus nad can be grounding for people. Journaling is also a great way to cultivate self expression.
